The founders of Functional Imperative came together over a unified passion for code and common business values; primarily, the understanding that passion driven success comes from adaptive learning and growth.

The founders brought these values to life with Lighthouse Labs - now Canada’s leading coding bootcamp - and the growth of the Functional Imperative team. The FI team consists of skilled and driven personalities from exceptionally diverse backgrounds; ranging from entrepreneurial pursuits, teaching music, stock trading, industrial engineering and everything in between. As a collective unit each team member comes together to connect people and businesses to the power of evolution through technology.
