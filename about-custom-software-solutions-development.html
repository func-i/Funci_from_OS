The founders of Functional Imperative came together over a unified passion for code and common business values; primarily, the understanding that passion driven success comes from adaptive learning and growth.

The founders brought these values to life with Lighthouse Labs (a leading web development bootcamp) and the growth of the Functional Imperative team. The FI team consists of skilled and driven personalities from exceptionally diverse backgrounds; ranging from entrepreneurial pursuits, teaching music, stock trading, industrial engineering and many things in between. As a collective unit each team member comes together to connect people and businesses to the power of evolution through bespoke software development.
