Senior Developer

Us

We build beautiful mobile apps, complex websites, and enterprise software. Our clientele spans a wide variety of fields including in the legal, healthcare, real estate, startup, and social impact verticals. What ties them together is the pursuit of creative solutions.

Founded by some of Canada's top software developers, Functional Imperative is focused on pushing the leading edge. As a true learning organization, it is mandate to empower our fantastic team to dream big and then get out of your way.

You

Experienced, hungry, quick to learn, good. You love making architectural and technological decisions. You're not dogmatic about languages or frameworks. You want autonomy to make things better.

Primary Duties

  • Own projects top to bottom, combining front-end UI with back-end server management. You are The Decider.
  • Work with the right technology for the job, probably starting your search from these options: Ruby, Rails, MiniTest, JS, AngularJS, Karma, React, Flux, Node.js, SCSS, CoffeeScript, PostgreSQL, MongoDB. You choose, so long as you can deliver on time and on spec.
  • Learn and use new technologies for your day-to-day work, experiments, or FI-supported side projects.
  • Support and collaborate with internal development team through code reviews, mentorship, discussing solutions.
  • Engage the development community through blog posts, meetups, speaking engagements.
  • Communicate honestly and transparently with clients and internal team.
  • Share the occasional dog .gif in Slack.
