Kevin Johnson
Developer

Kevin Johnson hails from a country rooted in the artisanal history of chocolate production since the early 17th century. As a Lighthouse Labs graduate, he devours any stack that may come in his way and conches his code to Belgian grade perfection. He has a passion for the functional paradigm of programming, with a special love for Standard ML; but really, he has a curiosity bordering on obsession with anything technology related.

Today he is a junior web developer, but heading toward tomorrow he is aspiring to be an expert in Neural Networks, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Analysis/Visualization. Kevin believes that the main ingredient to success is hard work and proper guidance infused in a competitive environment of highly talented and intelligent people.
