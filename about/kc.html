Kristin Cheverie
Developer

Kristin worked in Prince Edward Island for 7 years as a print graphic designer, before dipping her toes into HTML and CSS as a web designer. This sparked an interest in code, a move to Toronto and graduation from Lighthouse Labs in May 2016. Kristin is taking her passion for design and user experience and applying it to front-end development at Functional Imperative.
